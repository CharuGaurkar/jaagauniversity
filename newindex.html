    UNICYCLE

    The Self Balancing Electric Unicycle is a really interesting form of transportation. Instead of having 2 wheels like a hoverboard, it only has a single wheel, which is usually between 12 inches and 16 inches in diameter, with some outliers having a massive 18″ wheel. That’s why some people call it a one wheel scooter or Unicycle.
